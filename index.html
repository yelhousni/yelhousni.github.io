About

I am a cryptogrpahy engineer at ConsenSys (team gnark) and a PhD student at Ecole Polytechnique (team GRACE) under the supervision of François Morain and Aurore Guillevic.

My work at ConsenSys consists in developing a zero-knowledge cryptography open-source ecosystem in Go (see gnark and gnark-crypto libraries). My PhD research focuses on pairing-based zero-knowledge proofs and friendly related cryptographic primitives.

Interests

  • Zero-knowledge proofs
  • elliptic curves cryptography
  • pairing-based cryptography
  • privacy in blockchains
