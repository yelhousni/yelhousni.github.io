Biography

Youssef El Housni is a cryptogrpahy engineer at EY Blockchain Hub and a PhD student in Ecole Polytechnique. My work at EY focuses on developing cryptographic tools to address privacy and scalability issues in Ethereum blockchain. These tools include mainly zero-knowledge proofs, universal accumulators and proxy-reencryption schemes. My PhD research, under the supervision of Daniel Augot and François Morain in project-team GRACE/LIX, focuses on pairing-based zero-knowledge proofs and friendly related cryptographic primitives.
