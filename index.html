About

I am a cryptography engineer at ConsenSys working on a zero-knowledge cryptography open-source ecosystem in Go (see gnark and gnark-crypto libraries) and building Linea, an EVM-equivalent Type-2 zkEVM. I completed my PhD in Mathematics and Computer Science at Ecole Polytechnique. I was fortunate enough to be advised by three supervisors throughout my PhD journey: François Morain, Aurore Guillevic and Daniel Augot.

My research focus on efficient proof systems based on algorithmic number theory. I am particularly interested in the theory and application of bilinear pairings and elliptic curves in SNARKs.
